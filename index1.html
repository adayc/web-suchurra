Viaggio

Features

Viaggio is a website that allows you to make comparisons between the main services provided by interprovincial buses, and also shows a map with the route and stops. All this with the aim of providing comfort and safety to passengers who choose a bus.

Warmi Trip

Features

Warmi trip is a social network oriented only for traveling women. In this you can post photos as well as plan them with a budget and suggestions. In addition to this we offer an emergency button in case you find yourself in a dangerous situation and need help.

Features

FoodMap is an application that allows you to find restaurants near your location according to the area, among them we have typical food, fish and seafood, etc. Additional to this shows the location, capacity, payment methods of the restaurant, if it is petfriendly and its website.
